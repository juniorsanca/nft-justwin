About Doodle Jump

Jump is a platforming video game built for Web Browsers. In Doodle Jump, the aim is to guide a four-legged creature called "The Doodler" up a never-ending series of platforms without falling.

How to Play

  1. Save the falling doodler by moving him to a platform.
  2. Use the left and right arrow keys to control Doodler.
  3. If you hit the enemy or don't land in the platform on jump, doodler dies.
  4. Enjoy doodle jump !!
